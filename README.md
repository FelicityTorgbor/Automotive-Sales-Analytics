# Automotive-Sales-Analytics

## Project Overview
This project examines revenue trends, product performance, pricing behavior, order status, and regional sales contributions. The analysis highlights key drivers of revenue and identifies opportunities for market expansion and margin improvement.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Evaluate geographic revenue distribution.
- Identify top-performing product lines.
- Assess pricing effectiveness against MSRP.
- Examine order and deal-size trends.
- Generate business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Power BI – Dashboard development and visualization
- Microsoft Excel – Data preparation and cleaning
-DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs
- Power Query – Data transformation and preprocessing

## Key Insights
- Revenue reached $9.76M, exceeding the $5M target by 95%.
- France and the USA contributed over 94% of total revenue.
- Classic Cars and Vintage Cars generated the highest sales volumes.
- All product categories sold below MSRP, indicating aggressive discounting.
- Approximately 95% of orders were successfully shipped.
- Small deals accounted for over 70% of transactions, presenting upsell opportunities.


## Recommendations
- Expand marketing efforts in Spain and Australia.
- Review discounting strategies to improve margins.
- Investigate the January 2020 dispute spike.
- Bundle low-volume products with best-selling items.
- Explore emerging growth opportunities in Asia and Africa.

## Conclusion
The business demonstrates strong revenue performance but faces risks from market concentration and margin compression. Strategic diversification and pricing optimization can support long-term sustainable growth.
